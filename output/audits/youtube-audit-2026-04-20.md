## 5. Thumbnail Playbook (locked)

From your top-performing thumbnail (Top 5 Neighborhoods):

**Every future video thumbnail must have these 5 elements:**
1. **On-location** — playground, street, front yard. Never studio, never stock.
2. **Color-pop wardrobe** — pink/red/emerald shirt against blue sky or green trees
3. **Face expression: curious or concerned** — NEVER smiling. "What I found" energy.
4. **Neighborhood proof in background** — house, sign, street, park visible behind you
5. **Text real estate on one side** — dark pill overlay, one side of frame empty for text

**Text on thumbnail (max 4 words):**
- Shorts: Neighborhood name OR dollar anchor. One or the other, not both.
- Long-form: 3-4 word question or claim. Examples: "Don't Move Here", "$300K Won't Cut It", "Academy ISD Wins"

**Implementation:** lock these rules into `thumbnails/brand.js` and `thumbnails/config.json` so every generated thumbnail follows them by default.
