> **Purpose:** Convert TikTok viewers into Beehiiv newsletter subscribers, then nurture them into deal inquiries and clients.

## 1. The Funnel

```
TikTok Video (data teaser, 30-60 sec)
    |
    v
Viewer comments keyword "INVEST"
    |
    v
DM with newsletter signup link (manual or automated)
    |
    v
Beehiiv subscriber (The Temple TX Investor Brief)
    |
    v
Biweekly nurture (Deal Autopsy, market data, MTR breakdowns)
    |
    v
Deal inquiry ("I want to run numbers on a property")
    |
    v
Client (FUB intake, Action Plan 26: Investor)
```

### Why This Funnel Works
- TikTok's algorithm rewards comments and saves. A keyword CTA ("Comment INVEST") simultaneously drives algorithmic distribution AND captures high-intent leads.
- The newsletter filters tire-kickers from serious investors. Someone who reads biweekly deal breakdowns for 4-8 weeks is pre-qualified by behavior.
- Taylor's data-first voice is the moat. Generic agents cannot replicate real deal autopsies with actual P&L numbers.

### Conversion Targets
| Stage | Target | Metric |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Video views | 5,000-15,000/video | Average across investor content |
| Comments ("INVEST") | 2-5% of views | 100-750 per high-performing video |
| DM → Newsletter click | 40-60% | Strong because they asked for it |
| Newsletter subscribe | 70-80% of clicks | Low friction on Beehiiv |
| Subscriber → Deal inquiry | 5-10% within 90 days | Biweekly nurture warms them |

## 4. Comment-to-DM Automation

### ManyChat on TikTok (2026 Status)

ManyChat launched TikTok DM automation in late 2025. As of March 2026, it supports:
- **Keyword triggers in comments:** A user comments "INVEST" and ManyChat auto-sends a DM with the newsletter link.
- **Limitations:** TikTok's API restricts DM automation to users who have commented on your video within the last 24 hours. The DM must be sent within that window.
- **Cost:** ManyChat Pro ($15/month) covers TikTok automation.
- **Setup:** Connect TikTok Business Account to ManyChat. Create a flow: Trigger = comment contains "INVEST" -> Action = send DM with Beehiiv link + brief message.

### Recommended ManyChat Flow

```
TRIGGER: Comment contains "INVEST" (case-insensitive)
    |
    v
AUTO-DM (within 5 minutes):
"Here's the link to The Temple TX Investor Brief:
[Beehiiv URL with UTM]

Real deal breakdowns with actual P&L numbers.
Drops every 2 weeks. No fluff. -- Taylor"
    |
    v
FOLLOW-UP (24 hours later, if they didn't click):
"Did you get a chance to check out the investor brief?
The next issue drops [date]. This one covers [teaser]."
```

### If ManyChat Isn't Available or Gets Restricted

**Manual alternative (15 min/day):**

1. After posting, set a 2-hour timer
2. Open TikTok notifications, filter for comments containing "INVEST"
3. Tap each commenter's profile -> Send Message
4. Paste the template DM (save it in Notes app for quick paste):

```
Template DM:
"Hey [name] -- here's the investor brief link: [URL]

Real cash flow breakdowns on Temple TX deals.
Drops every 2 weeks. Next one covers [teaser]. -- Taylor"
```

5. Batch this during the post-filming wind-down on Tuesdays and again Wednesday morning
6. Track volume in a simple spreadsheet: date, video, comments received, DMs sent, clicks (from Beehiiv)

### Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
- Use ManyChat for the auto-DM on high-volume videos
- Manually DM anyone who asks a follow-up question or shows high intent (e.g., "I'm looking at a duplex in Temple")
- Manual DMs convert at 2-3x the rate of automated ones because they feel personal

## 5. Content Teasers: Newsletter Issue to TikTok

Every biweekly newsletter issue generates 2-3 TikTok teasers. This creates a content flywheel: TikTok drives subscribers, newsletter content fuels TikTok.

### Teaser Formula

```
TEASER STRUCTURE (15-30 seconds):
1. Hook: State the most surprising number from the newsletter (0-3 sec)
2. Context: Why that number matters (3-15 sec)
3. Blur: Tease the conclusion without revealing it (15-25 sec)
4. CTA: "The full breakdown is in this week's investor brief. Link in bio." (25-30 sec)
```

### Teaser Examples by Newsletter Section

**Deal Autopsy Teaser:**
- Hook: "The Deal Autopsy dropped today."
- Show the property photo, purchase price, rehab cost on screen
- Show the cash flow number... then blur/pixelate the verdict (pass/fail)
- "Was it a winner or a money pit? The full P&L is in the investor brief. Link in bio."
- Duration: 20 seconds

**Market Data Teaser:**
- Hook: "Temple TX inventory just did something it hasn't done in 14 months."
- Show a chart (hand-drawn on whiteboard or screen grab) with the trend line
- Circle the anomaly but don't explain it fully
- "I break down what this means for your next buy in the investor brief. Link in bio."
- Duration: 25 seconds

**MTR/Strategy Teaser:**
- Hook: "This property cash-flows $193/month as an MTR but LOSES money as a long-term rental."
- Show both scenarios side by side on screen
- "The difference is one strategy decision. I walk through the full model in this week's newsletter."
- Duration: 20 seconds

**Bell County Bulletin Teaser:**
- Hook: "Bell County just changed something that affects every investor's tax bill."
- Show the old vs. new number
- "If you're underwriting deals in Temple, you need to update your spreadsheet. Details in the investor brief."
- Duration: 15 seconds

### Teaser Posting Schedule

```
Newsletter drops: Every other Tuesday

Teaser 1 (pre-drop): Monday before newsletter
  -> "Tomorrow's investor brief covers [topic]. Link in bio to subscribe before it drops."

Teaser 2 (drop day): Tuesday (same day as newsletter)
  -> Deal Autopsy teaser with blurred verdict

Teaser 3 (post-drop): Thursday after newsletter
  -> Market data teaser pulling one surprising stat
```

### Repurposing Workflow (Tuesday Filming Block)

```
1. Film the video once (vertical, 30-60 sec)
2. Edit in CapCut (one master edit)
3. Export three versions:
   a. TikTok: Add TikTok-native captions, trending sound if applicable
   b. YouTube Shorts: Same edit, add "Subscribe" end card overlay
   c. Instagram Reels: Same edit, slightly different caption style

4. Post schedule:
   - TikTok: Publish natively (never via scheduler) at 11 AM CST Tuesday
   - YouTube Shorts: Upload same day, 2 PM CST
   - Instagram Reels: Upload Wednesday, 10 AM CST (stagger for freshness)
```
