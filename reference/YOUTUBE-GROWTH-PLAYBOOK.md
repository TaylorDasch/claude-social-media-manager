# YOUTUBE GROWTH PLAYBOOK — Real Estate / Hyperlocal
## Reference for skills that generate YouTube metadata, plan content, or suggest topics
## Updated: March 2026

---

## ALGORITHM FUNDAMENTALS (What Actually Drives Growth)

1. **Click-through rate (CTR)** — thumbnail + title are 80% of the game
2. **Average view duration (AVD)** — YouTube promotes videos that retain past 50%
3. **Session time** — videos that lead to MORE YouTube watching get boosted
4. **Subscriber conversion** — videos that convert viewers to subs signal quality
5. **Appeal → Engagement → Satisfaction** — YouTube's own framework. Optimize in this order.

---

## TITLE FORMULAS THAT WORK FOR REAL ESTATE

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| [NEGATIVE] + [LOCATION] | "Don't Buy a House in Temple TX Until You Watch This" |
| [SPECIFIC NUMBER] + [OUTCOME] | "$187K Rental Property: Full Cash Flow Breakdown" |
| [COMPARISON] | "Temple vs Belton TX — Which Is Better for Investors?" |
| [QUESTION] + [ANSWER TEASE] | "Is Temple TX a Good Place to Invest? (I've Done 100+ Deals)" |
| [YEAR] + [SPECIFIC TOPIC] | "Best Neighborhoods in Temple TX (2026 Update)" |
| [PRICE] + [WHAT YOU GET] | "What $350K Buys You in Temple TX (Full Tour)" |
| [PERSONA] + [SPECIFIC ANSWER] | "BSW Residents: Buy a House BEFORE Your First Day" |

### Title Rules:
- Always include "Temple TX" or the specific city
- Front-load the most interesting word (numbers, negatives, specifics)
- Under 60 characters for mobile display
- Never use ALL CAPS on more than 2 words
- Year-stamp time-sensitive content
- Affordable/price-specific titles consistently outperform (data from Taylor's channel analytics)

## DESCRIPTION STRUCTURE (7 Sections, Every Video)

1. **Answer-first paragraph** — data-rich, 2-3 sentences (this is what AI engines extract — 44.2% of ChatGPT citations come from the first 30% of content)
2. **Context paragraph** — why this video matters, who it's for
3. **Timestamps** — every section break (chapter titles should be question-format matching page H2s)
4. **Areas/neighborhoods mentioned** — with links to templetxhomes.net pages
5. **Key data points repeated as text** — redundant with video, intentional for AI parsing
6. **About Taylor section** — boilerplate with credentials
7. **Contact + links** — templetxhomes.net, Calendly, social

### Entity Declaration (MANDATORY — first 3 lines):
```
Hi, I'm Taylor Dasch, a REALTOR and active real estate investor with EG Realty in Temple, TX.
I've closed $27M+ in transactions and completed 100+ personal investment deals across Bell County.
```

## RETENTION ARCHITECTURE

- Pattern interrupt every 60-90 seconds (visual change, topic shift, question)
- Tease upcoming section: "In a minute I'll show you the red flags..."
- Put your strongest content at the 40-60% mark (where most drop-off happens)
- End with curiosity for next video (drives session time): "Next week I'm breaking down..."
- Use YouTube Analytics retention curves to identify weak moments → re-edit or avoid the pattern

## WHAT TO FILM VS. WHAT TO SKIP

### HIGH ROI (Film These):
- Deal of the Week (recurring, builds habit, investor lead gen)
- Neighborhood tours with commute tests (evergreen, high search volume)
- "Where NOT to invest" negative hooks (algorithm boost + trust)
- BSW-specific relocation guides (timed to Q4 and Match Day)
- Investor strategy explainers with Temple numbers (attracts OOS investors)
- City comparisons with real data (Temple vs Killeen, Temple vs Waco)
- AEO gap videos (fill data voids AI engines can't answer)

### LOW ROI (Skip These):
- "Day in the life" vlogs without analytical framing
- Generic market updates without specific data
- Listing tours that just describe what viewers can see
- "Top 10" lists without original data
- Anything a Zillow listing page already answers

## YOUTUBE SHORTS STRATEGY

- Repurpose the single most interesting 30-60 sec clip from every long-form video
- Shorts title = search-optimized (YouTube Shorts IS a search engine)
- Title MUST include "Temple TX" + keyword
- Description includes "Full video: [LINK]" to drive traffic to long-form
- Post Shorts 24-48 hours AFTER the long-form video (gives long-form a head start)
- Best Shorts: one shocking stat, one bold opinion, one "did you know"
- Heavy text overlays for numbers

---

## COMMUNITY POSTS (Underutilized Growth Lever)

- Post 2-3x/week between video uploads
- **Types:**
  - Polls ("Which neighborhood should I break down next?") — drive engagement metrics
  - Data drops (one stat + one sentence) — keeps channel active
  - Behind-the-scenes (filming day photos) — builds connection
  - Pop quizzes ("How much are property taxes on $400K in Temple?") — drives comments
- Community posts reach NON-subscribers — best free organic growth hack
- Include website link in every community post

---

## PLAYLIST STRATEGY

- Create playlists by persona: "For Investors," "For BSW Relocators," "Neighborhood Tours"
- Playlist titles should be search-friendly: "Investing in Temple TX — Complete Guide"
- End screens should point to next video in the playlist (drives session time)
- Pin your best playlist to channel homepage per audience tab

---

## END SCREEN + CARDS

- Every video gets an end screen: subscribe button + next video recommendation
- Cards at relevant moments (mention BSW → card to BSW video, mention investing → card to investing video)
- NEVER put a card in the first 30 seconds (kills retention)
- Cross-promote between channels: Living in Temple video mentions investment potential → card to Investing in Temple video

---

## YOUTUBE PREMIERES

- Set Deal of the Week as YouTube Premiere (scheduled live release)
- Premieres generate live chat during first viewing → YouTube treats like live stream engagement
- Significant algorithmic boost for zero extra effort
- Schedule for Tuesday mornings (Deal of the Week day)

---

## CHAPTER MARKERS (Add to All Videos)

- Format chapter titles as questions matching your page H2s
- Example: "0:00 Introduction | 2:15 What school district is Lake Pointe in? | 5:30 What are the negatives?"
- Chapters appear in search results → increase CTR
- Chapters create structured data AI models can parse
- Add retroactively to top 20 existing videos (2-3 hours total)

---

## GBP VIDEO POSTS

- Post 30-second clips from latest video directly to Google Business Profile
- Description should match your page's H2 questions
- Creates ANOTHER citation signal for Google's AI Overviews
- Most agents don't do this — easy differentiation
