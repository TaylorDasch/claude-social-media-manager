- 5-9 minute drive, straight up South 31st Street
- No highway required — all surface streets
- 38 minutes to Fort Hood (this is NOT a Fort Hood neighborhood)
**On-screen overlay:** Premium dark-ops location map (build with video-overlay-generator)
**B-roll:** Drive from Legacy Ranch entrance to BSW hospital parking lot — real-time with timer on screen
**Talking point:** "If you're a physician doing early morning rounds, this is one of the shortest commutes you'll find in all of Temple."

### SECTION 4 — WHAT YOU GET FOR THE MONEY (3:00-5:30)
**Topic:** Price ranges, home sizes, what $425K-$750K buys
**Key data:**
- Price range: $425,000 to $749,000
- Median: $460,000
- Median size: 2,555 sq ft
- Price per square foot: $153-$232 (median $184/sqft)
- All homes built 2021-2025 — everything is new
**On-screen overlay:** Price range card with stats grid
**B-roll:** Walk past 3-4 different homes showing variety at different price points
**Talking point:** "At $184 a square foot, you're paying a premium over the broader Temple market at $157. That premium is buying you new construction, Belton ISD, and the BSW commute."

### SECTION 5 — THE BUILDERS (5:30-7:00)
**Topic:** Arnold Design + Build (Phase 1) vs John Houston Homes (Phase 2)
**Key data:**
- Phase 1: Arnold Design + Build — completed, established homes
- Phase 2: John Houston Homes — active, 2,100-3,400+ sq ft floor plans
- Build quality reputation for both
**On-screen overlay:** Builder comparison card (two panels)
**B-roll:** Close-ups of construction quality, architectural details, exterior finishes
**Talking point:** "John Houston Homes is one of the more respected semi-custom builders in Central Texas. These aren't DR Horton cookie-cutters — you're getting real quality for your money."

### SECTION 6 — SCHOOLS (7:00-8:00)
**Topic:** Belton ISD — the premium driver
**Key data:**
- Belton ISD (Grade B, TEA score 80) vs Temple ISD (Grade C, TEA 77)
- Feeder: Charter Oak Elementary → Lake Belton Middle → Belton High School
- School boundaries redrawn 2024 — verify specific addresses via BISD Infofinder
**On-screen overlay:** School district card with feeder pattern
**B-roll:** Drive past Charter Oak Elementary
**Talking point:** "Belton ISD is the #1 reason families pay a premium to live here instead of the neighborhoods ten minutes east. That TEA score gap isn't huge on paper, but it drives pricing across the entire market."

### SECTION 7 — THE NEGATIVES / SCARS AND ALL (8:00-9:30)
**Topic:** What nobody tells you
**Negatives to cover:**
1. **No community pool** — Master plan prioritizes trails/green space instead. If you want a pool community, look at Bella Terra.
2. **Railroad border** — MK&T Railroad line runs along the eastern edge. Currently inactive, being converted to a hike/bike trail, but it's there.
3. **Tight lot sizes** — You're paying $460K but you're NOT getting acreage. Median home is 2,555 sq ft on a master-planned lot.
4. **STR restrictions** — HOA actively penalizes Airbnb/VRBO. If you're thinking MTR play, think again.
5. **Only 14 sales in the last 12 months** — Thin market. If you need to sell quickly, there's less comp data and potentially longer days on market.
**On-screen overlay:** Pros & Cons split-screen card
**B-roll:** Show the railroad area, lot sizes between homes, HOA sign
**Talking point:** "Here's the thing — I'd rather you know this now than find out after closing. The no-pool thing bothers some people. The lot sizes bother some people. But if the BSW commute and Belton ISD are your priorities, these trade-offs might not matter to you at all."

### SECTION 8 — WHO SHOULD BUY HERE (9:30-10:30)
**Topic:** Persona match
**Ideal buyers:**
- BSW physicians/staff who want the shortest possible commute
- Families prioritizing Belton ISD over price
- Buyers who want new construction (2021+)
- Premium budget: $425K-$750K range
**NOT ideal for:**
- Budget buyers under $350K
- Investors (HOA + STR restrictions + price point)
- Fort Hood-primary commuters (38 min drive)
- Anyone who needs acreage or a pool
**On-screen overlay:** Buyer profile card (3 boxes + "NOT for" box)
**B-roll:** Taylor walking and talking, direct to camera

### SECTION 9 — VERDICT & SUMMARY (10:30-11:30)
**Talking points:**

- "The premium you pay — $460K median vs $300K Temple median — is buying you three things: the commute, the schools, and the build quality."
