## Ground Rules Applied (per `feedback_no_long_term_claim_hooks.md` 2026-04-25)
- Every data point is verifiable today (0–12 month window) or sourced to an official document.
- No claims about long-term build quality, foundation/soil failure, year-8+ warranty erosion, or structural longevity were imported even when sources made them. Sources that contained banned-angle claims are noted.
- Where a builder's "warranty structure" is reported, only the contractual format (1-2-10, third-party administrator name, claim portal) is recorded — not predictions of how the policy will perform.

## Sources containing banned-angle claims (excluded from body)
- `templetxhomes.net/dr-horton/`, `templetxhomes.net/flintrock-builders/`, `templetxhomes.net/omega-builders/`, `templetxhomes.net/carothers/`, `templetxhomes.net/kiella/` — Taylor's own analyst pages contain commentary about post-closing service that crosses into long-horizon prediction.
