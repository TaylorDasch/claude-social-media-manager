## DEEP THINK — STRATEGIC ANGLE

### Why This Video Works Right Now (March/April 2026)

The timing is perfect for five reasons:

1. **Waco just got named a "demand-side decline" market** by Tactica RES. Not oversupply — actual demand erosion. That's a headline most investors haven't seen yet. You're the first YouTuber to connect this to a Temple comparison.

2. **Temple's correction is bottoming.** Down 3-4% YoY but with $810M+ in committed capital investment (Rowan data center broke ground March 2, 2026 + SeAH commercial production Q1 2026). You're buying the dip right before the catalyst hits.

3. **Waco's Magnolia tourism has been "treading water" since COVID** per the Waco Tribune. Tourist attendance at major attractions plummeted to ~1M in 2020 and hasn't recovered to pre-COVID peaks. The STR golden era may be over.

4. **Baylor enrollment softening** — graduate programs intentionally shrinking, overall enrollment flat-to-down. That's a rental demand headwind nobody is talking about on YouTube.

5. **Both cities are on I-35 at nearly identical price points** ($255K vs $218-280K depending on source), making this a true apples-to-apples "where should my next $60-80K go?" video.

### The Contrarian Angle (Your Edge)

Every other comparison video will say "Waco = Magnolia = appreciation" and "Temple = cheap = cash flow." That's the lazy take. Your angle is the opposite:

**Neither market cash-flows at 7.25% DSCR rates on a median-priced home.** That's the honest truth that will separate you from every other investor YouTuber. The REAL question isn't "which one cash-flows?" — it's "which one gives you the best TOTAL RETURN path over 5-10 years?"

Answer: Temple. And the data isn't close.
