## Speaking Notes

**Energy:** Analyst-with-an-edge. You're ranking things. Be confident. The whole point of this video is the **honest opinion.** Mealy-mouthed ranking = no engagement.

**Tone checkpoints:**
- Tier F: slowest, most deliberate. This is where controversy lives — retention peak.
- Tier S: confident, assertive. **"These ARE the best."** No hedging.
- Upset Pick: conspiratorial, "here's the hidden one" tone.

**Banned phrases:**
- "At the end of the day"
- "Every neighborhood has its pros and cons" (we're ranking — say the verdict)
- "It really depends" (define it and move on)
- "If I'm being honest" (you're always honest, don't signpost it)
