### FAQs

1. **Where is Fryers Bend located in Temple, TX?**
   > Fryers Bend is a gated luxury subdivision on the south side of Temple, Texas, accessed via Castlerock Drive and Iron Branch Road. The neighborhood sits inside a private creek-side park and walking trail, approximately 8 minutes from Baylor Scott & White Temple Main, 10 minutes from I-35, and 15 minutes from Temple airport.

2. **What's the median sale price in Fryers Bend (2026)?**
   > Based on the last 12 months of MLS data, the median closed sale price in Fryers Bend is $765,000 with a median of $251.58 per square foot. Five homes closed in the window with a price range of $695,000 to $939,309.

3. **Who is the primary builder in Fryers Bend?**
   > Eagle Ridge Builders (Hodges Eagle Ridge Builders, Inc.) is the primary builder in Fryers Bend, with Guy Fowler of Fowler Legacy Group listing most of their inventory. Eagle Ridge offers a 1-year builder warranty and 10-year structural warranty. The Mahler Group has listed select higher-tier homes in the neighborhood as well.

4. **Is Fryers Bend in Academy ISD or Temple ISD?**
   > School district lines in this footprint are not uniform. The most recent $998,000 MLS listing is tagged Academy ISD, but other lots may fall differently. Any home you are serious about, verify the specific address with the school district directly — do not rely on the MLS tag alone.

5. **How long do homes take to sell in Fryers Bend?**
   > Median days on market for closed homes in the last 12 months is 72 days. The range is 1 day (at-ask sale) to 129 days. Two of the last five closed sales went below list price — one at 5% under, one at 8.5% under — while three closed at list.

6. **How does Fryers Bend compare to Austin real estate?**
   > Per Redfin public data, West Lake Hills (78746) trades at $650–$900/sqft. Fryers Bend trades at $251/sqft. A 3,070-square-foot home in West Lake Hills would run approximately $2.3 million; the same square footage in Fryers Bend runs $765,000. That's a 3x spread for similar new-construction quality.

7. **What's included in an Eagle Ridge home in Fryers Bend?**
   > Standard finish includes GE Café appliances, 6-burner gas cooktops, custom cabinets, butler's pantry, beamed ceilings, iron front doors, and a fully equipped covered back porch with outdoor kitchen, fireplace, and beadboard ceiling. Most production builders in Central Texas offer only a covered slab — the outdoor kitchen alone represents $35,000–$45,000 in build cost.

8. **Is Fryers Bend a gated community?**
   > Yes. Fryers Bend has gated entry with a private park and walking trail along the creek inside the community boundary. The interior park is not open to the public.

9. **What's the commute from Fryers Bend to Baylor Scott & White?**
   > Approximately 8 minutes to BSW Temple Main campus. Commute times should be verified during an in-person visit, as traffic patterns vary by time of day.

10. **Who should I contact to see homes in Fryers Bend?**
