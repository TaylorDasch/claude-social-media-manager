## Production Notes

### Mac Moment Guidelines (applies to both versions)
- Physical comedy must come FROM the content (overreact to the actual content), not OVER the content (random antics)
- Deadpan reset must be genuine — Taylor stops everything and looks into the camera with no expression
- 5–8 sec total, not longer — TikTok rewards pacing
- Practice twice before take — the deadpan snap is what makes it work

### Audio / Music
- Licensed TikTok-native music ONLY (use TikTok's commercial library, not YouTube audio)
- Duck music under voice by 12-15 dB
- No music during Mac Moment — silence makes the deadpan hit harder

### Vertical Framing
- 9:16 throughout
- Taylor in center-third vertically
- Text overlays in upper-third (above face) or lower-quarter (above UI elements)
- Avoid text in center — TikTok UI overlays there

### Hashtags (3-tier per Content OS rules)
- Broad: #TempleTX #LuxuryHomes #AustinEquity
- Medium: #FryersBend #RelocationTexas #NewConstructionTX
- Narrow: #TempleTXRealEstate #AustinToTemple #CentralTexasHomes

### Posting Rules
- **Film natively on filming day** — do NOT repurpose the YouTube footage
- Use Taylor's phone + gimbal for the TikTok shoot, NOT the YouTube cinema camera
- The TikTok should feel like Taylor just stopped mid-walkthrough to tell you something — unpolished, fast, real
- Post same day as YouTube publish for cross-promo tailwind
- Add "Related Video" link in TikTok bio linking to the full YouTube tour — catches the high-intent spillover

### What NOT to Do
- ❌ "Hi I'm Taylor" — never. Cold open with motion + claim only.
- ❌ Don't repurpose YouTube clips — film TikTok-native
- ❌ Don't pitch physicians or investors on TikTok — buyers/relocators only
- ❌ Don't use the 27M volume or 100-transaction credentials on TikTok — too salesy for the platform
- ❌ Don't reveal the Austin math AND the $40K detail in the same TikTok — save one for a follow-up
