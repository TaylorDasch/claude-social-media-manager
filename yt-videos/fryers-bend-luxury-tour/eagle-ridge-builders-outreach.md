## If They Say Yes — Pre-Interview Prep

**3 honest questions to ask on camera (no sales pitch angles):**
1. What makes Fryers Bend different from your other Temple builds?
2. Who's actually buying here — what do the 9 sales in the last year have in common?
3. One thing buyers keep asking you that surprises you.

**Ground rules for your builder:**
- Honest answers, not marketing copy. Viewers can smell rehearsed.
- No committing to pricing claims they can't back up
- Let them wear what they normally work in, not a suit

## If They Say No

**Fallback plan:**
- Exterior drone + street-level only for occupied homes
- Find an active listing in Fryers Bend (check MLS active — some of those 9 sold comps may have new listings behind them) and feature that as the hero walkthrough with listing agent permission
- Still mention Eagle Ridge Builders by name in the script since they're the dominant builder in the neighborhood — just without on-camera partnership
