## Concept A — "Austin Equity" (A/B PRIMARY — Recommended)

### Visual Direction

- **Primary image:** Taylor facing camera 3/4-turn, in front of a Fryers Bend hero home exterior (representative Eagle Ridge build with iron door + board-and-batten clearly visible)

- **Attire:** EG Realty polo or casual-premium shirt

### Overlay Text

- Primary: **"$765K = $2.3M IN AUSTIN"**
- Layout: "$765K" on top line, left-aligned, emerald `#059669`, large bold
- "= $2.3M IN AUSTIN" on bottom line, right-aligned, state-flag blue `#002868`, slightly smaller
- 5 words total, mobile-legible at 200px width

### Color Treatment

- Hero exterior: evening-golden grade (warm tungsten tones, slight vignette)
- Sky: muted peach-gold
- Overlay emerald for Temple price (Taylor brand)
- Overlay blue for Austin price (state-flag reference, subtle)
- Thin white stroke outline on both price overlays for mobile legibility

### Why It Wins

- **Hooks Austin-exit persona in under 2 seconds** — they see "Austin" and "$765K" before processing anything else
- **Specific claim** (the 3x spread) is the whole video's thesis compressed into one frame
- **Mobile-legible price contrast** wins in cold feed scrolling
- **Taylor's analyst-pointing body language** reinforces his brand positioning — matches his on-camera voice as the "here's the data" agent, not a reaction-channel creator. Parasocial hook without selling.
