## Legend

- **Static** = tripod locked-off
- **Taylor OC** = Taylor on-camera
- **Graphic** = post-production overlay

### SOLO BACKUP Methods

- **S1 — Static-mount OC:** Taylor locks tripod, delivers line to camera, walks away for next shot. Walking B-roll cut in during edit.
- **S2 — Tripod-locked walking pass:** Camera on tripod; Taylor walks into frame, delivers, walks out. No gimbal operator needed.
- **S3 — Phone + gimbal self:** DJI Osmo Pocket 3 or iPhone on gimbal, arm's-length selfie walking. Works for short inserts only.
- **S4 — Drone auto-follow:** Drone in ActiveTrack on Taylor for walking approach shots. Requires Part 107 familiarity.
- **S5 — Skip & substitute:** If shot needs assistant, skip and substitute a B-roll equivalent at that timestamp.
