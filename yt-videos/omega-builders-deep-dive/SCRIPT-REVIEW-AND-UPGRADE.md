## Part 4 — 🎨 The Cottage Series Creative Anchor (1:45–4:15)

This is the one-creative-element-per-piece Taylor mandate. Everything else stays straight analyst voice.

### The Frame: "The Resident's First House"

We frame the Cottage Series through the lens of a PGY-1 medical resident starting at BSW on a $65K intern salary. Not "here's a starter home." Instead: "here's the exact house a first-year BSW resident can afford to buy on Day 1 using the Extraco physician loan."

This is the creative move: **price tier becomes a character.** The $249,900 Riley isn't a house, it's "the Day 1 house." The $360,900 Finley becomes "the Year 5 house" (attending-track).

### Full segment script

**VISUAL (1:45–2:00):** Drone aerial of Hartrick Ranch. Lot spacing visible. Country-edge-of-Temple feel.

**[ON CAMERA — exterior of Cottage Series model, Hartrick Ranch]**

## Part 5 — Other Surgical Fixes

### Fix 1: Spec comparison — add the DR Horton win row

After your Omega-wins rows, add:

> "One thing DR Horton does beat Omega on — raw square footage. At the same price point you'll typically get 200–400 more square feet from DR Horton. If maximum space per dollar is all you care about and spec quality is secondary, they're the play. But at equal square footage, Omega is cheaper per finish grade. That's the tradeoff."

**Why:** One concession = ten times the credibility. Otherwise the entire comparison reads like an ad.

### Fix 2: Consolidate CTA to ONE primary

Replace the final 45 seconds with:

> "If you want my free new construction buyer guide — every builder, every community, real tax rates, school district maps — comment the word NEWBUILD below. I'll DM it to you. That's the only thing I'm asking you to do. If you want to talk numbers, my phone's in the description. Taylor Dasch, EG Realty. I'll see you in the next one."

**Kills:** Instagram DM ask, website mention, the "call me or text me" stack.
**Keeps:** NEWBUILD comment (trackable, FUB-integrated), phone in description (warm leads only).
