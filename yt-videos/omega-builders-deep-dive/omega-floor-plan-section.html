Quick Answer

What floor plans does Omega Builders offer in Temple, TX?

Omega Builders offers 50+ floor plans across six series in Temple and Belton — Cottage (10 plans, starter homes from ~1,400 SF), Sterling (8 Texas-themed plans), Medallion (11 mid-tier flagship plans), Princeton (4 semi-custom plans), Windsor (4 premium two-story plans), and Medallion III (13 large two-story plans up to ~3,000 SF).

Buyers Miss This

The same plan name does not always mean the same plan. "Newport" in the Medallion Series is a single-story, 3-bed layout; "Newport III" in the Medallion III Series is a two-story with a game room and ~800 more SF. Same for Fuller/Fuller III, Florence/Florence III, Millstone II/Millstone III. Always verify the exact series before comparing quotes — it's where most buyer confusion happens.
