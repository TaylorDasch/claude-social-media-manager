## Speaking Notes for Taylor

**Energy:** Analyst, not salesperson. Think "I'll shoot you straight" tone. Zero hype.

**Pacing:**
- Hook: urgent, forward-leaning
- Zones 1-5: measured, data-first. One beat pause between price cards.
- Avoid zones: slower, deliberate. This is the highest-retention section per the research — DON'T rush it.
- Budget map: clean, list-style. No fluff.
- CTA: conversational. You're inviting, not selling.

**Breath marks (`|`):** Break natural breath points if a sentence feels too long. Don't read through a breath.

**Banned phrases while reading:**
- "Um, uh, like" — re-take if you slip
- "Right?" as filler
- "So basically…"
- "At the end of the day"

**If you fluff a line:** Keep going. Re-take the full paragraph in post. The script is written in paragraph blocks specifically for this — you can cut between paragraphs cleanly.

**Music:** Under-dialogue music only, ducking to -18dB when you speak. Your commenter on "Should You Move to Temple" specifically called out loud background music — do not repeat that.
