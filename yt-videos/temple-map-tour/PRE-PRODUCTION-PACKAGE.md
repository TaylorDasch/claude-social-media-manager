## §1 — Voiceover splice plan (the retention spine)

Five new VO drops re-recorded and spliced into the existing 20:22. **These are the highest-leverage edits in the entire package.** Without them, no amount of polish saves retention.

### Drop A — Replace cold open (00:00–00:18)
**Records over:** the current "Guys, if you are moving to Temple..." opener.

```
"Three things you're going to learn in this video.

The zone where I bought my own home.

The one zone I would never let my mom buy in.

And the fifty-thousand-dollar mistake almost every relocator makes in their first thirty days here.

Most agents won't say any of this out loud. I will.

Let's go."
```

**Why:** Triple open loop. Each line plants a "stay until I see this resolved" hook. Brain commits to watch-through.
**Filming:** Face-cam (see §2 Shot 1).

### Drop B — Splice at 5:00 mark (during West Temple, before Lake Pointe details)
```
"Quick reminder before we keep going — that fifty-thousand-dollar mistake I mentioned? It's coming up in Zone 4. Let me finish West Temple first."
```
**Why:** First retention save. Reactivates open loop #3 right when viewer would normally drift.

### Drop C — Splice at 10:00 mark (during Canyon Creek segment)
```
"And the zone I actually bought my own house in? It's literally on the screen right now. Watch where I land in a sec."
```
**Why:** Second retention save. Pays off open loop #1 progressively (you tease, don't fully reveal until end).

### Drop D — Pattern interrupt at 14:30 mark (right before East Temple)
**Filming:** 1.5 seconds of complete silence + black screen with white text:
> **"Most relocation realtors won't say what's coming next."**

Then cut to face-cam (see §2 Shot 4):
```
"I'm probably going to get crucified in the comments for this one. But I'm gonna say it anyway. East Temple."
```
**Why:** Hard pattern interrupt at the natural drop-off zone. Pays off open loop #2.

### Drop E — Receipts moment at 17:30 (during East Temple)
```
"Yes, I'm a Temple realtor. Yes, I'm telling you to walk East Temple in person before you buy. That's the difference between selling you a house and being your realtor."
```
**Why:** This is the quote that gets clipped and shared. Defines your brand in 12 seconds.

### Closing CTA — replace 19:47 outro
**Filming:** Face-cam (see §2 Shot 6).
```
"If this video helped you, here's how I work. I send you the actual list of homes — the ones that match what you actually want, in the zone that fits your life. Not Zillow links. Not 60 listings dumped on you. The list. Schedule a relocation call below. Or text me. Either way."
```

Film all 6 in one sitting. Half neutral background (office, plain wall), one outfit, one lighting setup. Lav mic. ~30 minutes.

| **4** | ~14:30 (East Temple pattern interrupt) | 4 sec | Drop D face-cam portion | Serious, no smile, direct |
| **5** | ~17:30 (Receipts moment) | 8 sec | Drop E above | Direct, slight conviction lean |
| **6** | 19:47 (Outro CTA) | 8 sec | Closing CTA above | Open, smile, friendly |

**Equipment:**
- Camera: phone in landscape on tripod is fine if light is good
- Audio: lav mic on you (do NOT rely on phone built-in)
- Lighting: window light or softbox at 45° front
- Wardrobe: solid color shirt, no logos that aren't yours
- Backdrop: keep it simple — bookshelf, plain wall, or your office

**Pro move:** record each shot 3 times with slight variations. You'll thank yourself in the edit.
