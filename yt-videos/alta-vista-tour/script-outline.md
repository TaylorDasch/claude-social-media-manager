### Segment 6: THE HONEST MOMENT — Scars & All (5:45–7:00)
**Visual:** Face to camera, standing in the neighborhood
**This is THE segment. 75 seconds of honesty. This is your moat.**

**Cover ALL of these:**

1. **Foundation Reality (40 sec):**
"Here's the thing nobody else is going to tell you. Bell County east of I-35 sits on expansive clay soil. In the summer when it's dry, the soil shrinks and your foundation settles. When it rains, the soil swells and pushes back up. Over time, that cycle causes cracks — you'll see them in drywall, around door frames, sometimes in exterior brick. It's manageable. You water your foundation with soaker hoses during dry months. But every buyer here needs to budget for a structural engineering inspection and understand this is ongoing maintenance, not a one-time fix."

2. **FM 93 Traffic (20 sec):**
"The exits from Alta Vista onto FM 93 don't have protected left turns. During rush hour, you're merging onto a 55 mile-per-hour road. TxDOT has no improvements funded for this stretch. Just something to be aware of."

3. **DOM Reality (15 sec):**
"Average days on market here is 96 days — that's 20 days longer than the Temple average. This isn't a neighborhood where homes fly off the shelf in a weekend. Buyers have negotiating room."
