## HONEST NEGATIVES (SCARS & ALL)

### 1. Foundation / Clay Soil (MANDATORY)
- Bell County east of I-35 = expansive "shrink-swell" clay
- Summer drought shrinks soil → foundation sinks
- Heavy rain expands soil → upward pressure
- Visible signs: drywall cracks, brick mortar separation, sticking doors, uneven floors
- **Mitigation:** Soaker hose watering program during dry months, structural engineering inspection

### 2. FM 93 Left-Turn Hazard
- Main exits onto FM 93 lack protected left turns
- During peak hours, merging onto 55mph traffic is sketchy
- TxDOT: No improvements funded as of March 2026

### 3. No Community Pool or Clubhouse
- HOA is cheap ($10-16/mo) but covers nothing beyond landscaping
- Tradeoff: Lions Junction Water Park is directly across FM 93

### 4. Average DOM Higher Than Temple Market
- Alta Vista: 96 days vs Temple: 76 days
- Not a fast-moving market — buyers have negotiating power

### 5. Flood Risk
- ~16.6% of properties have flood risk (First Street data)
- Check specific lots before buying
