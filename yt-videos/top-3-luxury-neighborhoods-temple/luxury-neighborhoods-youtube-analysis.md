## #1: LEGACY RANCH — "The BSW Doctor's Neighborhood"

### Neighborhood Profile
- **Location:** South 31st Street at FM 93, Temple 76502 — directly on the BSW Medical District corridor
- **Price Range:** $521K–$749K (active listings); median closed $460K
- **Price/SqFt:** ~$182
- **Lot Sizes:** Oversized suburban lots, 140 total planned (Phase 1)
- **HOA:** Yes — community pool, walking trails, planned retail/restaurant village
- **Builders:** Arnold Design + Build (Phase 1, semi-custom $449K–$685K, building since 1993) and John Houston Homes (Phase 2, plans 2,100–3,400+ sqft)
- **Architectural Style:** Contemporary Texas Hill Country — clean lines, stone/stucco, covered patios
- **Age:** Active new construction — Phase 1 nearly built out, Phase 2 selling lots
- **Inventory Turnover:** 14 closed sales trailing 12 months; active homes move in 45–90 days when priced right
- **School District:** Temple ISD
- **Standard Specs (Arnold):** Post-tension slab, full spray foam insulation, 10-ft ceilings (12-ft in living areas), granite throughout including bathrooms, custom cabinetry, tankless water heaters, full irrigation front/back, complete sod, rear privacy fence, smart home pre-wire

### Demographic Fit
- **BSW Physician Story:** This is THE physician neighborhood. 6–9 minute commute straight down South 31st — no highway, no traffic. A surgeon finishing a 14-hour shift is home in under 10 minutes.
- **Physician Mortgage Angle:** Extraco Bank offers 0% down, no PMI physician loans. A $650K Legacy Ranch home requires $0 down for a physician — impossible in Austin.
- **Amenities:** Community pool (families with kids), planned retail walkability, trails. Temple Regional Medical Center also nearby for dual-hospital households.
- **Why BSW Over Austin:** $650K in Legacy Ranch gets 3,200+ sqft semi-custom with a 3-car garage. That same physician budget gets a 1,800 sqft townhouse in Cedar Park.

### YouTube Content Potential — Score: 9/10
| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Search volume proxy | 9 | "Legacy Ranch Temple TX" is actively searched by relocators. Arnold Design + Build is a known name. Two builder phases = recurring Google intent. |
| Visual tour potential | 9 | Active model homes to walk, community amenities to film, new construction = pristine curb appeal. Arnold's spec homes are staged and available. |
| Storytelling hook | 9 | Semi-custom process (choose your floorplan, finishes, lot), two distinct builders with different price tiers, planned retail village. Not cookie-cutter. |
| Austin comparison | 10 | The dollar-for-dollar comparison is devastating. $650K here vs. $1.2M–$1.5M in Westlake/Lakeway for comparable square footage and finishes. |
| Surprise factor | 8 | Post-tension slab + full spray foam as STANDARD in a $500K home. Physician mortgage 0% down. Most relocators don't know this level of spec exists in Temple. |

### Video Concept
- **Title:** `Inside Temple's $650K Luxury Homes — 6 Minutes from BSW Medical Center`
- **Cold Open (8 seconds):** *[Standing in front of Legacy Ranch entrance sign]* "This home behind me — $649,000, semi-custom, spray-foam insulated, 3,200 square feet. In Austin, this floor plan starts at 1.2 million. I'm Taylor Dasch, and this is Legacy Ranch."
- **Segment 1 — The Drive:** Film the 6-minute commute from BSW Medical Center parking lot to Legacy Ranch front gate. Real-time with a timer overlay. "This is what your daily commute looks like."
- **Segment 2 — The Walkthrough:** Tour an Arnold Design + Build model. Hit the spec sheet hard — post-tension slab, spray foam, 12-ft ceilings, granite in every bathroom. "These aren't upgrades. This is standard."
- **Segment 3 — The Math:** Side-by-side comparison with a Zillow listing from Lakeway or Cedar Park at $1.2M. Same sqft, same bed/bath, half the lot, HOA fees 3x higher, property tax rate nearly identical. "Here's what $600K of savings actually looks like."
- **Austin Comparison Talking Point:** "A BSW hospitalist making $300K can buy a $650K home here with zero down on a physician mortgage. That same doctor in Austin is stretching for a $500K condo in Round Rock. Temple isn't the compromise — Austin is."
- **Soft CTA:** "If you're relocating to BSW and want to see Legacy Ranch in person, I'll walk you through the builder options and physician mortgage programs. Link's in the description — or just text me, 254-718-4249."

## #2: CLIFFS OF CANYON CREEK — "The Estate Play"

### Neighborhood Profile
- **Location:** Canyon Creek area, ~2 miles south of BSW Medical District, Temple 76502
- **Price Range:** $560K–$999K+ (Cliffs section); broader Canyon Creek starts at $300K
- **Price/SqFt:** $170–$185+
- **Lot Sizes:** 0.5–2.8 acres — one Tudor estate sits on 2.8 acres with creek bluff views
- **HOA:** Varies by phase (minimal in Cliffs section)
- **Builders:** Individual custom builds — no production builder. Each home is architect-designed.
- **Architectural Style:** Tudor-style brick estates, circular driveways, mature landscaping, stone accents, creek overlooks
- **Age:** Established 2000s-era customs — mature trees, established landscaping, no construction dust
- **Inventory Turnover:** 11 homes currently for sale across all Canyon Creek phases. Luxury customs averaging 100+ DOM. Buyer's market — 96% sale-to-list ratio.
- **School District:** Temple ISD
- **Notable Features:** Koi ponds, decorative gardens, creek bluff views, wooded lots, gated sections

### Demographic Fit
- **BSW Physician Story:** The chief of surgery, the department head, the specialist who's been at BSW for 10+ years. This isn't for the incoming resident — it's for the physician who's ready to put down roots with a custom estate.
- **Commute to BSW Main:** 5–7 minutes
- **Amenities:** Adjacent to Canyon Creek Golf Course area, mature tree canopy, privacy of acreage without rural isolation. Still inside Temple city limits.
- **Why BSW Over Austin:** A 2.8-acre estate with a custom-built Tudor home for under $900K. In Westlake Hills, the lot alone costs $1.5M. The land value arbitrage is staggering.

### YouTube Content Potential — Score: 8/10
| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Search volume proxy | 8 | "Canyon Creek Temple TX" gets searches. "Cliffs of Canyon Creek" is more niche but high-intent — people searching this already know about the area. |
| Visual tour potential | 9 | Tudor estates on acreage with mature trees, creek views, circular driveways. This films like a documentary. Drone shots over the creek bluff are cinematic gold. |
| Storytelling hook | 9 | "Old money Temple" — these are architect-designed customs, not subdivision homes. Each one is different. The 2.8-acre Tudor with koi ponds is a thumbnail magnet. |
| Austin comparison | 8 | Custom estate on acreage for under $1M vs. $3M+ in Austin's estate neighborhoods (Rob Roy, Barton Creek). Different buyer than Legacy Ranch — this is the "I want land" buyer. |
| Surprise factor | 7 | The 96% sale-to-list ratio is the hidden story. Sellers are negotiating. A relocating physician with cash has leverage here that doesn't exist in Austin's estate market. |

### Video Concept
- **Title:** `$900K Custom Estates in Temple TX — What Austin Can't Touch`
- **Cold Open (8 seconds):** *[Drone shot pulling back from a Tudor estate on 2+ acres]* "2.8 acres, custom-built Tudor, creek bluff views, 7 minutes from BSW Medical Center — $879,000. In Austin, this lot alone is 1.5 million. Let me show you what's inside."
- **Segment 1 — The Lots:** Drone footage showing the scale of these properties. Walk the property line of a 1+ acre lot. "This isn't a subdivision. These are individual estates on acreage — inside city limits."
- **Segment 2 — The Custom Details:** Walk through a listed property highlighting architect-specific details — custom millwork, imported stone, the elements you can't get from a production builder. "Every home in the Cliffs is one-of-one."
- **Segment 3 — The Negotiation Window:** Share the market data. 96% sale-to-list. 100+ days on market for luxury customs. "Right now, sellers in the Cliffs are negotiating. If you're relocating to BSW in the next 6 months, you have leverage that won't last."
- **Austin Comparison Talking Point:** "A colleague of mine sold in Rob Roy last year — $2.4 million for 1.5 acres. In the Cliffs of Canyon Creek, you get comparable acreage, a custom build, and change left over from a million dollars. Same quality of life, different decimal point."
- **Soft CTA:** "I've been inside most of these homes. If you want a private tour of what's available in the Cliffs, shoot me a text — 254-718-4249. I'll pull the off-market listings too."

## #3: VALOR ESTATES + BELLA TERRA EXECUTIVE — "The New Guard"

### Neighborhood Profile

**Valor Estates:**
- **Location:** FM 93 at South 31st Street, Temple 76502 (adjacent to Legacy Ranch)
- **Price Range:** $500K–$1M+ (estimated when homes complete; lots $69K–$595K)
- **Lot Sizes:** 3/4 acre minimum — side-entry garages required
- **HOA:** Yes (valorestateshoa.com)
- **Builder:** John Houston Homes (exclusive)
- **Architectural Style:** Estate-style new construction on premium acreage lots
- **Age:** Brand new development — lots actively selling
- **School District:** Temple ISD
- **Unique Feature:** Every street named after a local Bell County war hero killed in action. Military heritage baked into the development.

**Bella Terra Executive Tier (Carothers):**
- **Location:** North of FM 93 and South 31st Street, Temple 76502
- **Price Range:** $500K–$569K (Carothers Executive Homes tier)
- **Price/SqFt:** $195–$222
- **Lot Sizes:** Standard suburban with ARC (Architectural Review Committee) enforcement
- **HOA:** Yes — future pool, trails, playground, park
- **Builder:** Carothers Executive Homes (fourth-generation Central Texas builder)
- **School District:** Temple ISD (Phases 1 & 2), Belton ISD (Phase 3)
- **Inventory Turnover:** Median closed $391K across all tiers; Executive homes are the premium tier

### Demographic Fit
- **BSW Physician Story:** The young attending or fellow who wants new construction with land but isn't ready for a $750K custom. Valor's 3/4-acre lots give estate feel at a lower total investment. Bella Terra Executive is the "smart money" play — fourth-gen builder quality without paying custom prices.
- **Commute to BSW Main:** 7–10 minutes (same South 31st corridor)
- **Amenities:** Valor allows pools and custom backyards on 3/4-acre lots. Bella Terra has planned community amenities. Both feed into the South Temple growth corridor.
- **The Military Angle:** BSW is the region's Level 1 trauma center supporting Fort Hood. Military physicians, contractor families, and veterans resonate with Valor's war hero street names. This is emotional — not just real estate.
