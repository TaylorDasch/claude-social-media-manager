3\. YOUTUBE-TO-WEBSITE INTEGRATION STRATEGY  
The goal is "Multimodal Entity Verification." AI trusts content more when the text, video, and schema all validate the exact same facts.

The 1-to-1 Semantic Match: Use the output from your semantic-embed-mapper to embed the most mathematically relevant video directly beneath the H1 on your neighborhood and relocation pages.

The Contextual AI Wrapper: Never just drop an \<iframe\> on a page. Claude Code must wrap them in a specific div. Below the video, include an H3 titled "TL;DW (Video Summary)" with 4 bullet points. Beneath that, add a \<details\>\<summary\>Read Full Video Transcript & Data\</summary\> HTML toggle. This feeds 1,500+ words of hyper-relevant entity text directly to the AI crawler without ruining the UI for human readers.

Chapter-to-Anchor Sync: Ensure your YouTube video chapters exactly match the \<h2\> tags on your website. Deep-link your YouTube description directly to the specific anchor (templetxhomes.net/bsw-relocation\#commute-times).

The "Data Hub" / Deal Sub-Pages: For PropStream "Deal Breakdown" videos that don't fit a neighborhood page, have Claude Code auto-generate a dynamic template page (/invest/deals/\[video-slug\]) filled with the raw PropStream data table extracted from the video.

4\. CONTENT CALENDAR OPTIMIZATION  
Shift from a "social media" cadence to an "AI Engine Feeding" cadence. AI engines look for cross-platform consensus.

Monday: The Knowledge Anchor (Long-Form)

Publish 1 desk-filmable, data-heavy YouTube video.

AEO Action: Claude triggers transcript-to-schema-pipeline, immediately publishing the optimized blog to the site.

Tuesday: The Local Entity Signal

Extract a single chart/stat from Monday's video.

AEO Action: Publish a highly dense Google Business Profile (GMB) post. GMB is the foundational layer for Google's local AI understanding. Also post to X/Twitter (heavily crawled by AI for real-time sentiment).

Wednesday: The PAA (People Also Ask) Snackables

Publish 2-3 Shorts/TikToks extracted from Monday's video.

Rule: Each short must explicitly answer one specific question (e.g., "What is the Bell County tax rate?").

Thursday: The First-Party Data Push (Newsletter)

Send the cron-automated newsletter featuring the "Deal of the Week" (MLS command center data).

AEO Action: Publish a web-archived version of this newsletter on the site (/market-reports) so Perplexity/Google can index the unique proprietary deals.

Friday: The Freshness Pulse

AEO Action: Run the dynamic-freshness-injector script to push live MLS data updates to 3-5 existing site pages.

5\. ENGAGEMENT TACTICS FOR 2026 (Quality Proxies)  
AI engines use "Saves," "Bookmarks," "Dwell Time," and "Shares" as proxy metrics for factual quality. Likes/Comments matter far less to an LLM.

YouTube:

The "Screenshot Asset": Put your complex CAD data or commute maps on the screen for 4 seconds and literally say, "Pause the video and screenshot this map." (AI vision models read these slides, and pauses/rewinds signal massive utility to the algorithm).

AEO-Optimized Chapters: AI Overviews pull directly from YouTube chapters. Name chapters exactly how people prompt AI: e.g., instead of "Taxes", use "03:15 \- How much are property taxes in Temple TX?"

The "Data Sheet" Lead Magnet: Stop saying "Link in bio." Say, "I put all this CAD data into a Google Sheet, link is in the description." High outbound CTR to your owned domain is a massive authority signal to Google.

TikTok / Shorts:

Green Screen over Raw Data: Do not use polished Canva graphics. Use the green screen effect over the raw, "ugly" backend of PropStream, CAD, or your MLS. It visually communicates "insider data," spiking watch time as users try to read the screen.

The "Save For Later" Hook: "If you are PCSing to Fort Hood this year, hold down on the screen and save this video to your moving folder so you don't forget these zip codes." Saves are the \#1 algorithm driver for short-form.

Native Text SEO: Ensure keywords ("Temple TX Real Estate", "BSW Medical Relocation") are typed using the native in-app text editor and left on screen for the first 3 seconds so visual AI correctly categorizes the video.

Instagram:

Data-Dense Carousels: Convert your Monday market data into 5-10 slide carousels. Educational carousels have the highest "Save" and "Share" rate on IG, which Meta's Llama models use to determine authority.

Automated DM Funnels (ManyChat/Claude): "DM me 'BSW' and my AI will instantly send you my 2026 Relocation Guide." This drives massive direct engagement and forces users into your owned AEO-optimized website funnel.

The "Send to Spouse" CTA: Explicitly ask for the exact engagement metric that feeds the algorithm: "If you are debating moving to Central Texas, share this reel with your spouse right now."
